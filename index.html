Climate Change

WELCOME TO CLIMATE CHANGE

Climate change can be a natural process where temperature, rainfall, wind and other elements vary over decades or more. In millions of years, our world has been warmer and colder than it is now.

EFFECTS

Text:The consequences of climate change now include, among others, intense droughts, water scarcity, severe fires, rising sea levels, flooding, melting polar ice, catastrophic storms and declining biodiversity.

Info on Effects and Causes

CAUSES

Text:Increasing greenhouse gas emissions from human activity.These come from burning fossil fuels such as gasoline for driving a car or coal for heating a building. Clearing land and forests can also release carbon dioxide. Landfills for garbage are another source. Energy, industry, agriculture and waste disposal are among the major emitters.

SOLUTIONS

Text:Save energy at home

Much of our electricity and heat are powered by coal, oil and gas.

Walk, bike, or take public transport
